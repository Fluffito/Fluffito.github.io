Documentation

Why Use Aphelion?

Aphelion isn’t just a tool—it’s a little bit of fun for your browser. The extension lets you “bonk” away anything you don’t want to see, from memes to politics to spoilers. The playful features and custom sounds can make blocking content genuinely entertaining.

Some users find that hiding stressful or upsetting topics helps them relax online. While Aphelion isn’t certified by therapists, it can help you create a calmer, more positive browsing experience. Whether you’re a gamer, a student, or just a random parent who wants to keep the internet a little more peaceful, Aphelion is here for you.
