Frequently Asked Questions

What does Aphelion actually do?

Aphelion lets you hide words, topics, memes, or anything you don’t want to see online. You can also blur or replace images, mute sounds, and customize your browsing experience for maximum chill.

How do I install Aphelion?

Download the extension from the Home page, unzip it, and use your browser’s Load unpacked or Add extension feature. Full instructions are on the Docs page.

Can I block specific words or topics?

Yes! Just add any words or phrases you want to block in the settings. Aphelion will handle variants and sneaky spellings too.

Does Aphelion work on all websites?

It works on most sites, but some websites with unusual layouts or heavy scripts might not be fully supported. If you find a site where it doesn’t work, let us know!

Is Aphelion safe and private?

Absolutely. Aphelion does not collect or sell your data. All filtering happens locally in your browser.

Can I use my own images or sounds?

Yes, you can upload custom images for blocked content and even set your own “bonk” sound effects.

What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Edge, Opera GX, and Firefox are supported. More browsers may be added in the future.

Something broke! What do I do?

Check the Docs or Support section. If you’re still stuck, email Chiwuaqio@gmail.com and I’ll try to help.

Is it free?

There’s a free version with all the basics, and a paid version for unlimited bonking and extra features. See the Home page for details.
